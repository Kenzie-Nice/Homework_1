Looking at the World

Looking at the world itself, I feel like I am rather blind. There are many things to see about it, but often we are too absorbed in our tiny version of it and we lack the ability to see what is really out there. More so, in what way does one truly see the world? From the perspective of oneself? Of our own small world? The world in a general policy, such as society as a whole? Or rather from a complex standpoint of how one feels directly about the world and our place within it? There is too much to say and too little to really go from.
